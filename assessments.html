Dyslexia Screen Tests for Children

Dyslexia Screen Tests do not diagnose dyslexia but identify the possibility of having dyslexia.

Dyslexia Screen Tests for Adults

Dyslexia Screen Tests do not diagnose dyslexia but identify the possibility of having dyslexia.

Both tests can also form the first steps in deciding whether a full diagnostic assessment is warranted.

Full Diagnostic Report

A detailed written report consisting of recommendations for support. The assessment will look at general underlying abilities (visual and verbal reasoning skills), cognitive function (memory and processing) and literacy skills (reading, writing and spelling).

The report can be used to support an individual at school, college and university and in the workplace.
